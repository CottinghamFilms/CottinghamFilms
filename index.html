Cottingham Films

Cottingham Films is a group of kids who just like to film and ride thier bikes.

Founded in 2016 by Kyle Cottingham with the goal of making the sickest edit ever codenamed Edit0. They have been evolving their filming and riding skills over the past years and are only getting better.

The edit is set to launch september 1st 2020.

Cottingham Films also dabbles in a little bit of photagraphy all photos to the instagram account @2wheelsofftheground .

Operating out of Ottawa we aren't blessed with great landscapes and massive trail systems but we have many differnt riding and shooting locations. These locations include but are not limited to Hampton Park(Built and maintained by us), Camp Fortune and Gatineau Prison trails.
